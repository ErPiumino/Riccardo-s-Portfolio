Bottega Sonora
Bottega Sonora is a contemporary music ensemble, born with the objectives of dissemination and awareness of contemporary music through concert activities, audio/video recordings and promotion on social media.
As an artist, I helped them achieving their goals through the devolpment of a Brand Identity. We discussed the visual impact they wanted to have on the world, and everyday we perpetuate this philosophy.
