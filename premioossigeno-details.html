Premio Ossigeno

Project information

  • Category: Design
  • Client: Associazione Culturale M2000
  • Project date: 2024
  • Project URL: premiossigeno.it

Premio Ossigeno is a format dedicated to traditional arts and digital arts that have the environment and eco-sustainability as their theme. Published and unpublished works of art that deal with environmental issues or are made with eco-sustainable materials will receive special recognition. Premio Ossigeno is a showcase for young talents and already established artists whose works proposed on the web will also be exhibited in contexts and live events planned and organized during 2025. From 10 October 2024 until June 2025, Art and the Environment are on display.
